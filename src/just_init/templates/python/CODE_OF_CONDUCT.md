# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, and collaborative. Assume good intent, welcome
different perspectives, and focus feedback on ideas and code.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, intimidation, and publishing
private information without permission are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to `your.email@example.com`. Project maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions and may apply other consequences when
behavior violates this code.
